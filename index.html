Thought Drop is a mobile app that creates serendipitous message discovery as users go about their day. You can leave a public note, or a private one for your friends! See what people are saying around you, both at the present and in the past!

Build app in a team of 4 at Hack Reactor. In charge of maps and front end design. Technologies used: Ionic/Cordova, Angular, MongoDB, and NodeJS/Express

Vagabond
Vagabond is a social map creator that helps people generate custom, interactive maps to share with friends. The intuitive interface provides an easy way to create custom adventures for your viewers and provide your own commentary for each location. You can then share your creations with viewers, who are treated to a beautifully styled map and simple scrolling functionality that seamlessly leads them from place to place.

Vagabond is a full stack application written in Javascript and uses MapBox, Google Maps API, Node.js, Express 4.0, and Postgres

Ultimate Flashcard Appz
The Ultimate Flashcard app is a polished, basic app that does what a flashcard app is supposed to do: flip cards! Utilizing code from developer.android.com, this code has an animation card flipper that creates a realistic flashcard experiences for the users when they flip back and forth between words and definitions. This app has a built-in database so you can have as many flashcards as you need for your specific purposes. This app also has special built-in feature that allows you to edit your word and definition if you found a mistake in your initial data entry.

This app is fully build in native Android(Java). And yes I designed that sexy icon with Photoshop!
